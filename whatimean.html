What to expect

I use a very lean and efficient process throughout the duration of the project, by documenting all the planning and strategy of your idea in Trello. I then use a combination of different tools to bring your idea to life and as digital products are organic and always changing I like to build working prototypes to give you an idea of your final product. I use InvisionApp for most visual prototyping but will sometimes just begin coding your project as some of the interactions could be very detailed.

If you choose to move forward into the development stage I work with a number of developers or can help you find a solid development firm if your project is large and requires a team. Either way you are in good hands.
